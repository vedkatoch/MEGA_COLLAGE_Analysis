Handling
 Strings
 st.write() parses Markdown (e.g., passing "**bold**" will make the text bold).
 st.text() displays strings exactly as they are (e.g., "**bold**" will be shown as literal asterisks).

Data Types:
st.write() acts as a "Swiss Army knife" that works with DataFrames, JSON, exceptions, and Matplotlib/Plotly figures.
st.text() only accepts string-like inputs

Arguments:
st.write() allows you to pass multiple arguments at once (e.g., st.write("Hello", 5, my_dataframe)).
